A Few Words About Us

SpliceUp brings people together in thousands of cities to do more of what they want to do in life.

It is organized around one simple idea: when we get together and do the things that matter to us, we’re at our best. And that’s what SpliceUp does. It brings people together to do, explore, teach and learn the things that help them come alive.

example if you like travelling, then here meet fellow travel enthusiasts near you. Come to travel, to relive adventures, share photos from around the world and get first-hand recommendation on new exotic destinations. Find fun, laughs and maybe even a new travel buddy.

Here members can also make their community and can share photos, videos, reviews, experience with other fellow members. Members can also organize an event which will be temporary till the event is completed.
